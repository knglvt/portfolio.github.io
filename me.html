I'm a 23-year-old designer and illustrator, based in Sarajevo. Currently, I'm majoring in Graphic Design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Sarajevo. Influenced by surrealism, I enjoy incorporating chance and “random” selection into my work, often using printer/copiers to create compositions, combining digital and analogue imagery.

I love experimenting with techniques, my latest obsession being animation. I enjoy creating short whimsical moments that look like they’ve been taken straight out of a dream, an exploration of how nature and everyday existence can be surreal and ethereal. A big influence in my work is my love for early computer graphics from nostalgic films and video games.
